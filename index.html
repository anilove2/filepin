How it works

Simple enough for anyone

Four steps. Under a minute. No technical knowledge required.

1
Sign Up Free

Create an account and choose your username. It becomes your personal file address on FilePIN.

2
Upload Your File

Drag and drop any file — PDF, image, ZIP, document. It's stored securely in the cloud instantly.

3
Set a PIN

Choose a 4 to 8 digit PIN that protects the file. Only people who know the PIN can access it.

Copy your unique link. Share it or bookmark it. Open it anywhere — enter the PIN and download instantly.

PIN is the only key

Even if someone has your exact file URL, they cannot see, preview or download your file without the correct PIN. The link alone is useless.

⏱️
Self-destructing download links

When a PIN is verified, a secure signed URL is generated that expires in 60 seconds. Nobody can reuse or forward it after that window.

🔑
You stay in control

Change your PIN anytime. Delete a file with one click. Track every download. Nothing happens to your files without your action.
